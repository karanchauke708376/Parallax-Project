BIKING

At Escape Adventures, it’s no secret, we love mountain biking. You can’t blame us for wanting to try more trails and new destinations. When we come across something so good, we just have to share it. Mountain biking provides a unique and exciting way to experience the outdoors.

This adventure requires a good deal of physical stamina and endurance. It is a sport that involves bike riding usually off the road, mostly over challenging and rough terrain. Though, sometimes risky, the sport teaches one to trust his own skills and be self-reliable. It teaches a bike rider new skills like doing minor repairs and also to help others in eventuality.

Our Mountain Bike Tours are designed to accommodate riders from a variety of backgrounds. Some tours are intended specifically for more experienced riders, while many are suitable for those with little or no mountain biking experience.

PARA GLIDING

Bir Billing in Himachal Pradesh is renowned for being the ‘Paragliding Capital of India’. It is known to be one of the perfect and best locations for paragliding. Located in the vicinity of Kangra Valley, there are several operators in Bir Billing that offer short, medium, and long flying sessions.

The first-ever Paragliding World Cup in India was hosted at Bir in 2015. The cost of paragliding at Bir Billing starts at INR 2500.

Kamshet is one of the best places for paragliding in India. This splendid retreat is nestled about 45 km from Pune in Maharashtra. The place will remind you of the glorious past of the country.
